Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

Pasco Chamber of Commerce

The site will be named after Pasco, Washington

Site Purpose

This website will include business promotions, for those who are starting out in their marketing journey. We promote businesses to people in Pasco, as a means to improve the society. Events are also displayed here to increase the communication of Pasco's residents.

Scenarios

How can I join the Pasco chamber of commerce?

What are the names of the commerce directors to contact them?

What local events are occurring in Pasco this coming week?
